This would be logical since of a higher number of cases were reported this would increase the probability of possible deaths thus increasing the value of the target prediction of new deaths. Moreover, if there was an increase in deaths in previous days then it would be reasonable to infer that in the future this trend will continue and thus also contributing to a higher value of new deaths.

SHAP Dependence Plot ¶

The partial dependence plot shows the marginal effect one or two features have on the predicted outcome of a machine learning model.

It tells whether the relationship between the target and a feature is linear, monotonic or more complex.

The function automatically includes another variable that your chosen variable interacts most with.

Our aim is to predict covid deaths based on a time series analysis of the number of deaths per county provided influenced by the factors in our training datset.
